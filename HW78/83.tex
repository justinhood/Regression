\[y_{53}=l_{52}+b_{52}=316.0897+4.54049=320.6302\]

\[[y_{53}\pm z^*s]=[320.6302-1.645*27.8918,\ 320.6302+1.645*27.8918]=[274.74819,\ 366.5122]\]
